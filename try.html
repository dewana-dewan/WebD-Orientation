About US

Web Development Wing , Geekhaven

Web Development Wing , Geekhaven is responsible for all Web-Development related activities conducted in the campus of IIIT Allahabad. It also encourages students to explore their creativity with the tools of Web Development.

What Do We Do ?

Web-Related Activities For Effervescense, Aparoksha and College Websites

Web Development Wing is Hyperactive during the Annual Cultural Festival , Effervescence and Annual Technical Festival , Aparoksha. It also maintains the college website, IEEE Website and many other websites.

Conduct Workshops And Create Projects

Web Development Wing also conducts workshops to help students understand the basics of web development . It also works on projects contributing towards the welfare of the institute and the society.
